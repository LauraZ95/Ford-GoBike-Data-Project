Ford GoBike (Jan. 2019 - Dec. 2019)¶

Introduction:¶

Ford GoBike is a regional public bicycle sharing system in the San Francisco Bay Area, California.

The bikes can be unlocked from one station and returned to any other station in the system, making them ideal for one-way trips.

The following data summary will focus on various aspects of the Ford Gobike system in 2019.

I hope to summarize different aspects of data to help this shared bicycle system better understand customer needs and optimize the physical system.

Main features of interest:¶

I am interested in the usage habits of the client groups of the Ford Gobike system. These habits include the way of use, purpose of use and time of use, etc.

The main focus was on the start time (semester, quarterly, monthly, day of week, hourly).
